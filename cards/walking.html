Brisk-Walking

Brisk walking is a moderate-intensity exercise that can be beneficial to your health12. It involves walking at a pace of 50-70% of your maximum heart rate, which can be calculated by subtracting your age from 220 beats per minute (bpm)1. Brisk walking can help reduce the risk of heart disease, diabetes, and some types of cancer2. It also has a positive impact on brain health and can aid in weight loss2.
